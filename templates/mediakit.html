Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in your collaboration packages?

Each package includes professional content creation, strategic posting at optimal times, authentic product integration, brand mentions in captions, and detailed performance analytics post-campaign. You'll also receive content drafts for approval before posting.

How long does it take to create content?

Typical turnaround is 7-10 days from product receipt to posting. This includes content creation, editing, your review/approval, and scheduled posting. Rush campaigns can be accommodated with 3-5 day turnaround for an additional fee.

Do you accept gifted products or only paid collaborations?

I primarily work with paid partnerships to ensure the highest quality content and dedicated time. However, I'm open to discussing product gifting for exceptional products that align perfectly with my audience, typically combined with affiliate partnerships.

What kind of analytics do you provide?

After each campaign, you'll receive a comprehensive report including: reach, impressions, engagement rate, video views, saves, shares, profile visits, and link clicks. I use Instagram/TikTok Insights and trackable links to provide accurate data.

Can we use your content for our own marketing?

Yes! Content usage rights are included in all packages. You can repurpose the content for your website, ads, social media, and other marketing materials. Extended usage rights and exclusivity options are available for discussion.

What's your audience demographic?

My audience is predominantly male (89.9%), aged 25-34 (48.3%), interested in gaming, tech, and PC building. Top countries include Brazil, USA, and India. They're highly engaged tech enthusiasts with purchasing power for premium products.

Do you offer package deals or long-term partnerships?

Absolutely! I offer discounted rates for multi-post campaigns and ongoing partnerships. Long-term collaborations (3-6 months) receive special pricing and priority scheduling. Let's discuss your goals to create a custom package.

What makes you different from other influencers?

I focus on authentic, quality content over quantity. My audience trusts my recommendations because I only promote products I genuinely use and believe in. Combined with professional video production, strategic marketing knowledge, and proven ROI, I deliver results that matter.
